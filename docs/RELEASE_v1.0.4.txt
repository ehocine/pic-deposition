PIC Deposition Benchmark Suite v1.0.4
======================================

Physics validation expansion (Tier 1 + Tier 2).

Changes since v1.0.3
--------------------
- Extend SimulationResult with per-step kinetic and field energy histories.
- Add initializeWarmLangmuirWave for finite-temperature Langmuir modes.
- New physics_studies module: time-series export, quasi-1D two-stream,
  Langmuir convergence sweep, Landau damping, multi-seed replication,
  and production-scale timestep profile.
- Add pic_benchmark flags --physics and --timeseries.
- Archive new CSVs: physics_timeseries, langmuir_convergence,
  two_stream_quasi1d, landau_damping, validation_multi_seed, production_sim.
- Add figures fig17--fig23 and TeX summary tables in plot_results.py.
- Fix run_all_benchmarks.sh validation archive glob (exclude multi_seed).
- Add pic_test smoke tests for warm Langmuir, Landau, and quasi-1D two-stream.
- Regenerate archived benchmark CSVs and physics validation data on Apple M1 Pro.
